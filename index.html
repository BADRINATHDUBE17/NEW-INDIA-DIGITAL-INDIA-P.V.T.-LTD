About NEW-INDIA-DIGITAL-INDIA-P.V.T.-LTD

NEW-INDIA-DIGITAL-INDIA-P.V.T.-LTD is a leading provider of banking, government, and non-government services in India. The company was founded in 2025 by two entrepreneurs, Mr. Badrinath Dubey, with the goal of providing quality banking and financial services to the rural population of India. Since then, we have expanded our services to include government and non-government services. NEW-INDIA-DIGITAL-INDIA-P.V.T.-LTD started its operations with a small team of 20 employees and has now grown to a team of over 200 employees.

NEW-INDIA-DIGITAL-INDIA-P.V.T.-LTD offers a range of banking services such as aadhar based payment system, cash deposit, money transfer, aadhar pay, and account opening. The company also provides government services such as filing of income tax returns, PAN card application, and passport application. NEW-INDIA-DIGITAL-INDIA-P.V.T.-LTD also offers non-government services such as bill payment, mobile recharge, and utility bill payment.
